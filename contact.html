Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Log in to your account and go to the "Orders" section to view tracking details.

Q: What is the response time for email support?
A: Email inquiries are typically answered within 24 hours on business days.
